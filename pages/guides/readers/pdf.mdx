## PDF Reader
Kavita provides a basic PDF reader experience that mimics Firefox's pdf reader, with some enhancements like Dark mode. 


### The Reader
    <Image
      src={readerLayout}
      alt="reader layout annotated"
      height={400}
    />
> 1. Open Table of Contents and Page previews
> 2. Hand Tool (for panning the document)
> 3. Text Tool (for text selection)
> 4. Enable immersive reading mode (fullscreen, no menus)
> 5. Toggle between laying out pages vertically or horizontally
> 6. Change layout: Single, Left Right, Right Left 
> 7. Dark/Light Mode
> 8. Close Book
